#Phishing Email Analysis Report – Paytm Scam Email
## Objective
To analyze a phishing email impersonating Paytm and identify common phishing indicators.
### Sample Email
From: support@paytm-securityverify.com
Subject: Urgent: Verify Your Paytm Account
Dear Customer,
We detected unusual activity in your Paytm account. Please verify your account immediately using the link below:
https://paytm-account-verification-secure.com
Failure to verify within 24 hours may result in account suspension.
Regards,
Paytm Security Team
________________________________________
### Analysis
1. Suspicious Sender
The sender uses a non-official domain that attempts to resemble Paytm.
2. Fake Verification Link
The URL does not belong to Paytm's official website and may lead to a credential-stealing page.
3. Urgency Tactic
The email creates panic by threatening account suspension within 24 hours.
4. Generic Greeting
The email uses "Dear Customer" instead of the recipient's actual name.
________________________________________
### Phishing Indicators Identified
•	Suspicious sender address
•	Fake website link
•	Urgent language
•	Generic greeting
•	Credential theft attempt
________________________________________
Conclusion
The email is a phishing attempt designed to trick users into revealing their Paytm login credentials. Users should verify sender details, avoid clicking suspicious links, and access services only through official websites or applications.
